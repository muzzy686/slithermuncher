What is Hextris?

Hextris is an innovative puzzle game inspired by the classic Tetris but with a hexagonal twist. It challenges players to rotate a hexagon to match colors and prevent blocks from reaching the center.

The game progressively speeds up as you play, testing your reflexes and strategic thinking in an addictive gameplay loop that's easy to learn but difficult to master.

How to Play

Controls

  • Use ← and → arrow keys (or swipe) to rotate the hexagon
  • Match 3 or more blocks of the same color to clear them
  • Prevent blocks from reaching the center of the hexagon

Scoring

  • Clear blocks to earn points
  • Chain reactions earn bonus points
  • The game speeds up as your score increases
  • Game ends when blocks reach the center

Tips & Tricks

Looking to improve your Hextris score? Here are some strategies to help you excel:

  • Focus on creating chain reactions for maximum points
  • Plan your moves ahead - look at the upcoming blocks
  • Try to keep all sides of the hexagon relatively clear
  • When in doubt, prioritize clearing the side with the most blocks
  • Practice rotating in both directions to improve your reaction time
